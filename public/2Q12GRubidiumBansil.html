Thursday is a good day since it's the day before Friday, which means the weekend is just around the corner. You've already gotten through the worst part of the week by Thursday and can begin to unwind. You've likely completed most of your work, so there's less anxiety. It's a breather before the weekend, and you can begin thinking about fun activities for tomorrow.

Another thing Thursday is awesome at is that it's an all-right day to socialize. Most people organize happy hours or just spend time with friends after class. You're not exhausted yet, so you still have some energy to have fun. Also, it's less tense than Monday or Wednesday, so you can manage to remain efficient and creative.
